# WatchVault - Luxury Watch Portfolio Intelligence

Welcome to WatchVault! This is a comprehensive platform for managing and analyzing your luxury watch collection.

## 🎯 What is WatchVault?

WatchVault is a sophisticated portfolio management system designed specifically for luxury watch collectors. It combines:

- **Collection Management** - Catalog your watches with detailed specifications
- **Portfolio Analytics** - Visualize your collection's value and composition
- **Market Intelligence** - Track market trends and valuations
- **AI Advisor** - Get intelligent insights and recommendations
- **Deal Discovery** - Find curated acquisition opportunities
- **Professional Appraisals** - Generate formal documentation

## 🚀 Getting Started

### First Time Use

1. **Create Your Vault**
   - Enter your name and email
   - Choose a unique name for your collection vault
   - Click "Enter Vault" to begin

2. **Add Your First Watch**
   - The welcome modal will guide you to add your first watch
   - Fill in details like brand, model, purchase price, and condition
   - Upload an image URL (optional) to visualize your piece

3. **Explore the Modules**
   - **Collection**: View and manage all your watches
   - **Portfolio**: Analyze value distribution and trends
   - **Market**: Track market values and appreciation
   - **AI Advisor**: Get personalized recommendations
   - **Deals**: Discover acquisition opportunities
   - **Appraisal**: Generate professional reports

## ❓ FAQ

**Q: Will my data be lost if I close the browser?**  
A: No! Your data is automatically saved and will be there when you return.

**Q: Can multiple people use this on the same device?**  
A: Yes! Each person can create their own vault and sign in/out as needed.

**Q: What happens to my data when I sign out?**  
A: It's safely stored and will load automatically when you sign back in.

**Q: Can I export my data?**  
A: Currently, you can generate PDF appraisal reports. Full data export coming soon!

**Q: How accurate are the market values?**  
A: Market values are AI-generated estimates. For formal appraisals, consult a professional.
